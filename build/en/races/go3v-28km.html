Go3V - 28 km

A race for running enthusiasts looking for a challenge. Go3V trail will lead you over the summit of Mt. Škabrijel and through the cavern on Holy Mountain. Finally, you will take a raft over the Soča river and reach the ridge of Mt. Sabotin.
