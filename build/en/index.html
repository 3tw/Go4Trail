A Race Between the Past and the Future

The peaks of Mt. Škabrijel, Mt. Sabotin and Holy Mountain delimitated the line of the Isonzo Front during the World War I. It is here that we set our trails. From the begining we wanted to unite people where they had been separated not long time ago.
