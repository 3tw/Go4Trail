About

Tek treh vrhov (T3V) started as all good things do. With a group of friends doing together what they like the most. We love to run. Especially when the trail goes uphill. That is why we decided to bring you a unique running experience. From the picturesque ridge of Mt. Sabotin and forest paths on Mt. Škabrijel to the cavern of Sveta Gora and stunning views of the Soča river. We wanted to capture the diversity that our homeground has to offer. And what better way to experience it than by going on a journey across three summits.
